🔗 Circuit Establishment
Before data transfer, a dedicated physical path is established through network switches. Request packets traverse the network to find and reserve a route. Once established, this path is exclusive for this communication.
📊 Bandwidth Reservation
The circuit reserves fixed bandwidth on each link in the path. This ensures constant quality but means unused bandwidth cannot be shared with other connections, leading to potential inefficiency.
⚡ Data Transfer
Once established, data flows continuously through the dedicated path. No routing decisions needed per packet. Low latency and predictable performance, ideal for real-time voice communication.
