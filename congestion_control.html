🚀 Slow Start
Begins with cwnd = 1 MSS. Window size doubles every RTT (exponential growth) until it reaches the slow start threshold (ssthresh) or detects congestion.
📈 Congestion Avoidance
After reaching ssthresh, growth becomes linear (increases by 1 MSS per RTT). Continues until packet loss is detected, then reduces window size.
⚠️ Fast Recovery
On packet loss: sets ssthresh = cwnd / 2 and cwnd = ssthresh. Quickly recovers without going back to slow start, maintaining higher throughput.
